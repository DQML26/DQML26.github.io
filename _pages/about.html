Welcome to DQML 2026


The DQML 2026 conference brings together researchers working on molecular dynamics, quantum effects, and machine learning in materials science and computational chemistry.

The meeting will be held in St. Johann in Tirol, Austria at the Explorer Hotel Kitzbühel from the 2nd of February to the 5th of February 2026.
