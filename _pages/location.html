Conference and accomodation

The conference will be held in St. Johann, in the Tyrol region of Austria.
The structure hosting the conference, which is also the accomodation, is the Explorer Hotel Kitzbühel.
The address is Speckbacherstraße 87, 6380 St. Johann in Tirol, Austria


Leisure activities

The hotel is located within walking distance of the St. Johann ski resort which has over 43km of slopes suitable for both beginner and advanced skiers.
Additionally, a daily ski bus service runs from St. Johann to the neighbouring ski slopes. The bus service is free of charge.
The are arround the hotel has plenty of affordable ski hire points, such as the Top Skiever Leih Mair and so acquiring equipment should be a breeze.
